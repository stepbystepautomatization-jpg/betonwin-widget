=== BANK TRANSFER & PAYMENT PROOF (COMPROBANTE) ===

How to make a deposit via bank transfer
1. Go to My Account > Cashier > Deposit > Bank Transfer
2. You will see the bank account details (account number, bank name, reference code)
3. Make the transfer from your bank using those exact details
4. IMPORTANT: Include the reference code in the transfer description
5. After completing the transfer, upload your payment receipt (comprobante) via chat or email

How to send your payment receipt (comprobante)
After making a bank transfer, you must send proof of payment so we can credit your account:
- Option 1: Upload it directly in the chat widget (click the attachment icon)

- The receipt must show: date, amount, sender name, and transaction reference number

What information must the comprobante include

- Date and time of the transaction
- Amount transferred
- Sender name (must match your BetonWin account name)

How long does it take to credit a bank transfer

- Weekends and holidays: up to 24 hours
- If more than 24 hours have passed, contact support with your Player ID and the receipt

I sent my comprobante but the deposit is still not credited
1. Verify the transfer was completed successfully in your bank app
2. Make sure the receipt clearly shows all required information
3. Check that the sender name matches your BetonWin account name
4. Contact support via chat with your Player ID and the receipt — we will verify it within 4 hours

BetonWin bank account details
The bank account details for deposits are shown in My Account > Cashier > Deposit > Bank Transfer. These details may change periodically. Always use the details shown in your Cashier at the time of deposit — do not use old details from previous deposits.

**MISSING CONTENT — Add to KB:**

```
=== DEPOSIT NOT CREDITED — TROUBLESHOOTING ===

Step-by-step: My deposit is not showing in my account

Step 1 — Check the payment method processing time:
- Credit/debit card, e-wallets: Should be instant. If not credited after 5 minutes, contact support.
- Cryptocurrency: Wait 10-30 minutes for network confirmations (check the blockchain explorer for your transaction).
- Bank transfer: Allow 1-3 business days. Weekend transfers may take until Monday.
- Mercado Pago / Directa24: Usually instant, but can take up to 2 hours during peak times.

Step 2 — Verify the payment was completed:
- Check your bank app or payment provider to confirm the transaction status is "completed" (not "pending" or "processing")
- If the status is "pending" in your bank, the transfer has not been sent yet — wait for your bank to process it

Step 3 — Check for common issues:
- Did you use the correct bank account details? (details change periodically)
- Is the sender name the same as your BetonWin account name? Deposits from third parties are not accepted
- Did you include the reference code in the transfer?

Step 4 — Contact support with:
- Your Player ID
- Payment method used
- Amount and currency
- Date and time of the transaction
- Screenshot of the payment confirmation or receipt

We will investigate and credit your account within 4 hours (business hours) or 24 hours (weekends).

Why was my deposit delayed?
Common reasons for delays:
- Bank processing times (especially international transfers)
- Weekend/holiday processing (banks don't process on non-business days)
- Missing reference code in the transfer
- Name mismatch between bank account and BetonWin account
- Cryptocurrency network congestion (more confirmations needed)
- Payment provider maintenance or system updates
```

The site is slow or not loading
1. Try the alternative URL: https://beton691.online

4. Disable any VPN or ad-blocker that might interfere
